The Numbers behind Movies and TV Shows

Sreeram Veerapaneni

  • Revised Project Proposal
    • As someone who watches lots of movies from various different languages,
    I get asked a lot of questions about what I think about certain movies.
    Sometimes, a movie that gets bad reviews will still be entertaining to me.
    The idea behind the project is to compare and contrast different movies,
    their gross, budgets, and other data as well as the production companies to
    see what factors influence the success of a movie or show and to see what movies
    and shows are higly profitable.
